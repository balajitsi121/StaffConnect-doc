StaffConnect — Laravel Admin Panel Pages

This document contains all pages and modules required for the Admin Panel of the StaffConnect platform, based on the hotel staffing workflow and future multi-industry expansion.

1. Dashboard

  • Total Hotels (Businesses)
  • Total Workers
  • Active Jobs (Shifts)
  • Pending Applications
  • Pending Timesheets
  • Payouts this week
  • Platform Revenue
  • Verification pending
  • Recent activity logs

2. Hotel Management (Business Management)

  • Hotels List
  • Hotel Detail Page
    • Profile details
    • Verification documents
    • Payment settings
    • Jobs posted
    • Applications received
    • Timesheets pending
    • Payouts generated
    • Reviews received
  • Hotel Verification (Approve / Reject)
  • Business Account Actions (Suspend / Reset password / Edit)

3. Worker Management

  • Workers List
  • Worker Profile Page
  • Worker Document Verification
  • Skills & Industry Mapping
  • Account suspension / Activation

4. Jobs (Shifts) Management

  • All Jobs List
  • Job Details Page
  • Job Monitoring (force close / reopen / assign worker)
  • Job Timeline

5. Applications Management

  • Applications List
  • Application Details
  • Manual worker assignment
  • Rejection & notes

6. Timesheets Management

  • All Timesheets
  • Timesheet Details Page
  • Approve / Reject / Adjust hours
  • Dispute evidence & resolution

7. Payments & Payouts

  • Payouts Overview
  • Completed / Pending / Failed payouts
  • Stripe Connect Account Status
  • Manual Payout Override
  • Payment Errors Resolution

8. Reviews & Ratings

  • Worker Ratings
  • Business Ratings
  • Remove abusive reviews
  • Rating dispute handling

9. Verification Center

  • Worker Document Verification
  • Business Document Verification
  • Centralized document review workflow

10. Industry & Skill Management

  • Industries List (Hospitality, Healthcare, Events, Logistics, etc.)
  • Skills per Industry
  • Add / Edit / Remove skills
  • Future industry scalability

11. Platform Settings

  • Payment Settings (fees, payout cycle, cancellation rules)
  • Email Templates
  • SMS Templates
  • Push Notification Templates
  • Job Rules (default radius, limits)
  • Verification requirements per industry

12. Reports & Analytics

  • Revenue reports
  • Payout reports
  • Job fulfillment statistics
  • Worker performance
  • Business retention
  • Dispute insights
  • Export CSV / PDF

13. Admin User Management

  • Admin Users List
  • Roles & Permissions
  • Activity Logs
  • Login History
